Last weekend, The Sonic Squirrels competed at the Pacific Northwest Championship, earning 105 district points and the Autonomous Award for our outstanding programming.

The district points we earned at this last competition as well as our past two district events have qualified us to go to the World Championship in Houston, TX.

We can't wait to see everyone there!!
