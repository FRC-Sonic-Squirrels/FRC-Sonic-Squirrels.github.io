Wanna donate to the Sonic Squirrels? All you need to do is 7 simple steps!

  1. Click here
  2. Sign in or Regester an Account using the Websites Criteria
  3. Click "Items At Student's School"
  4. Click Robotics
  5. Scroll Down to Robotics donations to Club/Hay, and enter in the amount you wish to donate
  6. Click Buy
  7. Scroll up to Checkout, and complete the transaction

If you have any questions, contact us at sonicsquirrels@gmail.com

THANK YOU!
