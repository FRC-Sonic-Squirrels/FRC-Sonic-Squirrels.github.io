Mentors are an integral part of our team. They teach us new things and guide us in the process of building a robot. Anyone can mentor a FIRST team, for example, our mentors come from many different backgrounds and walks of life.

Melanie got involved 2015 driving and feeding Squirrels as a parent, then a little more involved in 2016 helping mentor some of the "non-engineering" aspects of the team. Her son graduated but she stayed with the team as the operations mentor. She coaches the Business, Media, Spirit, Outreach, and Scouting sub teams as well as assistant Mr. Wilson with team logistics.  When not Squirreling around, she works full time as a Practice Manager at Redmond Eye Doctors. In her rare time off, she likes to be outside - preferably near a beach or castle but her backyard hammock is nice too.

John Ireton is Kaitlyn Morgan's father, and this is his first year. In the real world, he is a mobile software developer working on iPhone apps for Fortune-500 companies, so naturally he is mentoring on the Programming team. Many years ago, he used to play Magic: the Gathering competitively and was a certified judge. He also takes care of a small herd of backyard goats, chickens and one noisy duck.
